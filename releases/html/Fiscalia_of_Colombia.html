      EDITOR NOTES
      Published simultaneously with Enlace Hacktivista

      5 terabytes of emails hacked from the Office of the Attorney General of Colombia (Fiscalía General de la Nación) by Guacamaya, which has been linked to numerous kinds of corruption, including withholding evidence, ties to drug trafficking and paramilitary networks, espionage, acts of political persecution and murder.

      The Fiscalia issued a statement on Oct. 19, saying they opened an investigation about the security incident.
