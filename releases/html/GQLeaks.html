      EDITOR NOTES

      120 GB of emails hacked from the Centro Nacional de la Informatización de la Administración Pública de Guinea Ecuatorial (CNIAPGE) or Equatorial Guinea's National Center for the Computerization of Public Administration. The emails are dated between 2017 and early 2022.

      The CNIAPGE was founded in 2013. In 2015, Microsoft signed a contract with them. Oracle, ZTE and SafeNet now owned by Thales are also listed as partners on the center's website.

      Due to considerable amounts of PII and medical information in the dataset, the data is only being offered to journalists and researchers at this time.
