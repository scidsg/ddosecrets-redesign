Photographs of classified documents leaked by U.S. Airman Jack Teixeira from the 102nd Intelligence Wing of the Massachusetts Air National Guard. Teixeira posted transcribed documents and photographs of documents to a series of Discord channels.

Note: The information in the documents consists mostly of summarized intelligence reports, with some brief analysis. While invaluable to the public for the insight it gives into current events and the decisions of public figures and government, the information is not always accurate or fully contextualized. The information was recent when it became public, but it is not up-to-date.
